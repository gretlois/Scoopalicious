The Big Picture

The Goal

Scoopalicious is a budget-friendly ice cream shop that aims to provide delicious, high-quality treats at affordable prices for students, kids, and families. The business offers a variety of flavors, sizes, and combo deals to suit different budgets, along with exciting promos such as “Buy 1, Get 1” and discounts to attract and retain customers. Scoopalicious will use social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok to promote its products and create a strong online presence. The shop is designed to be fun, clean, and visually appealing, encouraging customers to visit and share their experience. With friendly staff, convenient payment options like cash and GCash, and a focus on customer satisfaction, Scoopalicious aims to become a go-to ice cream destination in the community.
