Spreading the Word

Marketing Strategy

The our scoopalicious will use social media to promote the our ice cream shop. We will post pictures and videos on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. to show our colorful ice cream and happy customers. We will also give promos like “Buy 1, Get 1” and discounts for kids and students. We will make sure our shop attractive and fun so people will take pictures and share online. We will also give the free taste samples to attract customers. During special days like birthdays and holidays, we will offer special flavors and packages.

Sales Strategy

The our Scoopalicious will offer affordable prices so students, kids and families can buy our ice cream. We will have different sizes like small, medium, and large to fit every budget. We will offer combo deals, such as ice cream with toppings or drinks, to encourage customers to buy more. We will also offer a buy one take 1, to repeat customers. And our shop will ensure that Our staff will be friendly, making customers feel welcome and comfortable so they will want to come back. In addition, we will accept different payment methods, including cash and g-cash for the convenience of our customers.
