Operating Plan

Scoopalicious is a dessert shop that's all about the community and making good desserts. We want to make sure that every day we do things in a way. When we make each scoop of ice cream we do it the same way every time. This way the ice cream is always the texture and our customers are always happy. We make our ice cream in batches so we have to check the quality at the start of each day. This is very important to us. Most of the time people get frustrated with dessert shops because the quality is not good or the service is slow when it is busy or the shop is not clean. Scoopalicious does things differently. We make a plan every morning to make sure we are doing everything right. We want to make sure our customers get service even if it takes a little longer. We check our freezers to make sure they are working right and our toppings are always fresh. This way we can focus on making our customers happy. We do this because Scoopalicious wants to make sure our customers have a time when they visit us at Scoopalicious.
