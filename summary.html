Business Summary

Scoopalicious, owned by Jonierose Fernandez, is a budget-friendly ice cream shop dedicated to serving delicious, creative, and high-quality treats to students, kids, and families. We offer a variety of flavors, sizes, and combo deals to fit every budget. Our goal is to create a fun, clean, and welcoming environment with friendly staff and a strong social media presence. We aim to be the go-to ice cream spot in the community, making happy memories with affordable treats.
